Cyberpunk

Cyberpunk, a dystopian genre warning against a "high tech, low life" future as unchecked corporate power and dehumanising technology run rampart. This all to closely mirroring a possible trajectory we could go down, where tech megacorps wield immense influence, invasive digital surveillance is commonplace, and ai integrates into daily life amidst vast social inequality
While we lack flying cars or widespread cybernetics, the foundation socio-political situation slowly shifts in to the genre; the online safety act and its equivalents in the EU and US, adaptation of facial recognition technolgies as seen in china and being rolled out in the west.

Studies have shown such as from MIT that using LLM's what we call AI's uses significantly less brain activity than normal web browsing say looking at code examples from stack overflow albeit self learning has the highest. To me a higher average brain activity correlates to larger amount of new neural connections but draw your own conclusions.
